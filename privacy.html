Privacy Policy

Siddur Hayom (סידור היום)

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Itamar Zerem ("we", "us", or "our") manages information in the Siddur Hayom mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account via Firebase (Email/Password), we store your email address and an encrypted password to provide access to your personalized data.

User Profile: You may optionally provide a display name.

User-Generated Content: We store data regarding synagogues and schedules that you manually add to the app.

Location Data: The app may request access to your GPS location only to help you find nearby synagogues. This data is used locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. If you choose to sign in using Google or Apple, your information is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

We do not sell your data.

We do not use tracking or advertising services.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We keep your account data as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" button within the app profile settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at: itush30@gmail.com
